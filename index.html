INDIA-Treasure of Spices


Spices A spice is a seed, fruit, root, bark, or other plant substance primarily used for flavoring or coloring food. A spice may be available in several forms: fresh, whole dried, or pre-ground dried. Generally, spices are dried. Spices may be ground into a powder for convenience. A whole dried spice has the longest shelf life.

India contributes to 75% of global spice production. This is reflected culturally through their cuisine. Spices are distinguished from herbs. Some most commen spices which are used in daily life-

Turmeric

Turmeric, a ground spice, has an earthy supporting flavour. Turmeric is a great anti-inflammatory, this one has huge health benefits and an astounding yellow colour.

Black Pepper

A strong immune system is important for helping you avoid illness, and black pepper can help here as well.Black pepper is a good source of manganese, a mineral that can help with bone health, wound healing, and metabolism.
